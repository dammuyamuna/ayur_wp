The Meaning of
AYURIMA

"Ayurima" is a fusion of two meaningful elements: Ayur and Ima.

Ayur comes from Sanskrit, meaning life or longevity. It represents holistic wellness, natural healing, and the ancient traditions of Ayurveda that nurture vitality from within.

Ima signifies essence, source, and a nurturing presence — symbolizing care, balance, and inner strength.

Together, AYURIMA represents the essence of life — a philosophy rooted in natural balance, well-being, and mindful living.
